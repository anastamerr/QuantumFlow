Hadamard Gate

Beginner
Overview:

Hadamard Gate - The Hadamard gate creates equal superposition.

Operation: H|0 = (|0 + |1)/2 H|1 = (|0 - |1)/2 Matrix: H = 1/2 * [[1, 1], [1, -1]] Properties: - Hermitian (H = H) - Unitary (HH = I) - Self-inverse (H = I) The Hadamard is essential for creating quantum superpositions.
